Fresh Cranberry Sauce

Description

This sauce is a must have for Thanksgiving! Easy to make and perfect alongside turkey.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • White sugar
  • 1 package of fresh cranberries

Steps

  1. Boil water and add sugar
  2. Stir in cranberries and bring to boil for 10 minutes
  3. Pour sauce into a dish and cover until use
  4. Mix sauce with a fork before serving
