Grandma's Cornbread Dressing

Description

This recipe is from my Grandmother, she loved making it for the holidays every year!

Ingredients

  • Cornbread
  • Vegetables
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Stock
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and place cornbread in a bowl
  2. Melt butter and add onions and saute
  3. Add onions to cornbread. Stir in stock, eggs, sage, salt, and pepper
  4. Bake in oven until golden.
